TOM SHELBY FROM PEAKY BLINDERS ARTWORK

In this page I will show all the process behind this

SKETCH

In most of the artworks I make, I always start with the sketch to bring clarity for the design.

Sketching

In this case it shows how I started first by making a simple sketch, to later be perfected and obtain what will be the final product in black and white.

Painting

Once the sketch is finished in detail, it is time to compare the colors that combine perfectly for the final artwork.

Final Artwork

Finally it has been finished and finally the details such as the background phrase and the final colors were added, as well as a change in the final size.
